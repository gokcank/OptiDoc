# OptiDoc - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 3, 2026

Hasan Gökcan Kahraman ("Developer") provides the OptiDoc ("App") as a free, ad-supported application. This SERVICE is provided by the Developer at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

**Information Collection and Use:** OptiDoc does not collect, save, or sell any personal data that could directly identify you (such as your name, phone number, or email address) on its own servers.

The core functionalities of the app, including document scanning and Optical Character Recognition (OCR), are performed entirely on-device (offline) using the Google ML Kit infrastructure. Scanned documents and extracted texts are stored locally on your device's storage.

**Advertising (Google AdMob):** To keep the app free, we use the Google AdMob advertising network. AdMob may automatically collect and share technical data, such as Device IDs or Advertising IDs, to serve personalized ads and perform analytics.

**Other Usages:** The App does not contain any cloud storage servers, external backup processes, or background servers. The security of the files (PDF, TXT) generated and exported from the App is solely the user's responsibility.
